2016 agenda:

13:10 - 13:20

Opening Remark by Martin Wong, Associate Dean of Engineering College

Session One: Academia VS Industry: How do scientists transform the world?

Scientists who ask right questions at the right time can make history and change the world. As the ultimate goal of scientific research is to improve the human condition, the transfer and commercialization of technology innovation serves the interests of both inventors and society. The 2016 UIUC U.S.-China Innovation & Development Forum will bring together inventors, entrepreneurs, corporate leaders and UIUC professors to discuss the way transforming the world by next generation of technology innovations.
