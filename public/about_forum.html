About 2016 UIUC US-China Innovation & Development Forum

On the Cutting-Edge of Innovation

The UIUC Innovation and Development Forum focuses on the cutting-edge issues of innovation, technological development and inspired industrial production.

In 2014, the President’s council of advisors on Science and Technology published the Report to the president Accelerating U.S. Advanced Manufacturing 2.0, which addressed securing the nation’s competitive advantage in transformative emerging technologies. In China, President Xi Jinping highlighted the importance of strengthening “structural reform of the supply front” to increase the quality and efficiency of supply system and provide a growth impetus for sustainable economic development in China. Two of the most impactful countries, U.S. and China, both rely their strategy on technological innovation.

In UIUC, at April 16th, with the topic of “Technology and Development”, the forum provides extensive discussion and direct interaction with leaders and pioneers from various fields. These events would offer unique perspective to the UIUC community and beyond to help develop a big picture of the new direction of technology innovation and the corresponding influence to both the personal and society development. The Innovation Forum, which aims to provide participants an invaluable opportunity to directly interact with leading figures from academic, industrial and investment field, will bring together leading thinkers and practitioners from around the world. Not to mention, the Innovation and Development Forum destines to provide a platform for Chinese and non-Chinese young generation to meet with potential partners for future career takeoff. The Forum is designed for people who want to envision what next generation of innovation will be like. Attendees will participate in a full day of stimulating, interactive discussion and inspiration.
