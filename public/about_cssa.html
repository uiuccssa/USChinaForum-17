About CSSA at UIUC

The Largest Chinese Student Community in the Central U.S.

The Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CSSA) is the official organization serving overseas Chinese students and scholars at UIUC through social, cultural, and professional activities in the community. As the largest registered student organization in UIUC with 352 enthusiastic members, CSSA hosts many events throughout the school year to bring the student body together. Some of their biggest events during the fall semester include:

Each semester we organized several dozen events. Some highlights from the past Fall Semester are:

CSSA Moon Gala

CSSA celebrated the Mid-Autumn festival by hosting a talent show in Foellinger Auditorium. As many as 1,500 people attended the show, including the Provost of UIUC, Consul General of China in Chicago, and many other high profile guests and professors.

CRRC Seminar

The seminar was hosted in collaboration with China Railway Rolling Stock Corporation (CRRC; largest train builder in the world; Number of Employees: 175,700) and China General Chamber of Commerce - USA. Many high profile guests including the Dean and Associate Dean of the College of Engineering, Vice President of CRRC, President of Bank of China America Branch, Consul General of China in Chicago attended the seminar, in addition to the 200 student participants from the university.

International Career Forum

In collaboration with Business Career Service (BCS), Engineering Career Service (ECS) and Career Center, CSSA hosts job fairs targeting the international students and providing job opportunities to hundreds of students in UIUC. Over years, UIUC CSSA has obtained great reputation and close relations with the university, as well as considerable influence over Chinese and international population in the entire university.
