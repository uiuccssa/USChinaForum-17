About UIUC

University of Illinois, or U of I

UIUC has a long historical culture of innovation, and is often regarded as a world-leading magnet for engineering and sciences (both applied and basic). It was in this university that the plasma display, the LED, the JavaScript, and the integrated circuit, among other key technologies, were developed. As a leading hub of high-tech innovation and development, UIUC is also the alma mater of many outstanding entrepreneurs who founded the NetScape, the AMD, PayPal, YouTube, Yelp, Oracle, Mosaic, Safari, Firefox, TeslaMotors, and Occulus Rift, etc. It is worth noting that the UIUC has a long relationship with China, and is one of the universities in the nation that has the largest number of Chinese students. In 2015, the number of registered Chinese students and scholars reaches 5295, with 40.4% of those are graduate students.
